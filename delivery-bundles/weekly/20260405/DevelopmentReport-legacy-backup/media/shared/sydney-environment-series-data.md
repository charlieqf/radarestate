## Episode Logic

Episodes 1-4 each isolate one environmental signal:

- flood
- bushfire
- heat
- tree canopy

Episode 5 combines them into a playful environmental-comfort ranking.

## Important Framing

- These are public-data screening signals, not official hazard certifications.
- Avoid claiming exact parcel safety or danger.
- Use suburb or precinct names as visual storytelling anchors, not deterministic outcomes.

## Shared Visual Story Pattern

1. Show Sydney-wide signal count
2. Highlight 3-5 representative precincts
3. Explain why this matters in everyday life terms
4. End with a simple memorable line
